# 🌱 Focus Timer App

**Author:** David

A simple and motivating productivity app that helps users stay focused using timed work sessions, rewards, and progress tracking.

## 📘 Overview

The **Focus Timer App** encourages productivity by allowing users to set custom focus sessions. After each completed session, users earn rewards like growing plants 🌱, motivational feedback, and celebration effects.

The app also tracks progress locally so users can build streaks and monitor their total focus time over time.

## ✨ Features

* ⏱️ Custom focus timer in minutes
* 🌱 Focus garden that grows after each completed session
* 🎉 Confetti celebration when a session ends
* 🔊 Reward sound effects
* 💬 Motivational productivity quotes
* 📊 Progress tracking with streaks and total focus time
* 💾 Data saved automatically using `localStorage`

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. Enter a focus duration in minutes.
2. Click **Start Focus** to begin the countdown.
3. Stay focused until the timer finishes.
4. Earn rewards and watch your garden grow 🌱
5. Use **Reset** to clear the current timer while keeping saved progress.

## 💾 Data Storage

The app uses **browser localStorage** to save:

* Total focus time
* Completed session streaks
* Earned plants/rewards

Your progress remains saved even after refreshing or closing the browser.
